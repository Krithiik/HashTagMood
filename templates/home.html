Tweet Analysis

We provide you with the trend in tweet count on a keyword or hashtag over the time period of last 7 days for each hour variation as a timeline chart. Apart from this the most recent and popular tweets are provided with their username, a glimpse of the tweet itself and link to that tweet with several other content. This content is also available to be dowloaded in excel file. From the recieved tweets the sentiment analysis is also done categorizing them as postive, negative and neutral tweets in a pie-chart

Personal Interest

From the tweets that the user liked, using Natural language processing, the tweets are categorised into categories and the user's recent interest domains are shown.

Targeted ADS

Given a bussiness account or any account, the last 10 tweets posted by the user are considered and the users who liked them are analysed and ranked describing their interest in the post posted by the bussiness account. The bussiness admin can use this report to promote their product directly to those users by using the username and the account link provided from our analysis.
